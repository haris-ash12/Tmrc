Microsoft Dynamics for Textile and Apparel
Description

A vertical solution built on Microsoft Dynamics platform. With solid, industry-specific operation and seamless integration with other Microsoft products, Microsoft Dynamics for Textile and Apparel is a thorough ERP (enterprise management solution) that delivers a high amount of scalability and easily adapts to your business needs and changing commercial structure.

The blend of the powerful software, a worldwide presence and the adoption of Microsoft’s “Cloud Based” Technique secures a risk free and low-cost implementation. It also supports multiple languages and currencies that can help you operate properly across geographies.

Features

Increase agility with versatile processes for manufacturing, sourcing and delivery allocation: Benefit from the built-in PLM and collaboration, multi-level manufacturing, neutral bills of materials and purchase requests and allocation simulations to meet changing distributor and customer requirements.

Manage complication for complete traceability: Handle several materials and product parameters effectively; using multi-dimensional item coding monitoring from raw material to finished goods. Also track materials through adjustable sourcing and manufacturing operations, logistics inventory and subcontractors.

Take control of costs and success: Implement industry specific information into financial management. Also get insight into brand collections’ or seasonal performance.
