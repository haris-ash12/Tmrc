Microsoft Dynamics for Banking Industry

In most companies, especially in these challenging financial times, a competent and optimized Accounts Payable team which can process its workload with reduced resources and higher productivity levels is a problem. A programmed process, making sure an easy throughput from bank statements to resolved invoices allows companies to give attention to the other important tasks. Organizations that are successful in this field gain the benefits associated with resource efficiency and reduced costs by using Microsoft Dynamics for Banking Industry. Therefore, it makes a big change. In a very speedily changing environment, agility is also very essential. To attain agility, you will need the support of your business program you can trust.

In short, Microsoft Dynamics for Banking industry helps the importing and computerized settling of bank statements. It also includes the Accounts Receivable, Accounts Payable and Standard Ledger modules.

Features
  • Use of lender accounts to recognize vendors and customers
  • Settlement to start invoices, predicated on invoice figures and amounts
  • Additional identifiers can be configured by users
  • Default ledger accounts e.g. Standard bank Costs, Payment Terminals
  • Ending balance features to make sure completeness
  • E-mail payment specs for larger payments
Benefits
  • Streamline bank processes
  • Automatic pay out of wide open invoices
  • No manual keying of standard bank statements or errors
  • Significant cost reduction
  • Automatic project to ledger accounts
